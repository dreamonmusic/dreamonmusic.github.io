    JOB - LORE


    ”Lore" means traditional knowledge, and the learning passed on in this song may be that we are essentially repetition. But repetition both receives and transforms. Repetition generates divergence, so the only thing inevitably returning is difference. What is eternally returning is not the same, but the new.

    Written, performed and produced by Sami Suova
    Artwork by Ben Sifel
    Released by Dream On 2024.

    THE EMBASSY - E-NUMBERS


    E-Numbers explores a nihilistic position. ”I don’t want the world to change. I just want to be against. Three cheers for us.” But the force and the beauty of the composition points further, to an overcoming of nihilism by affirmation of creative power. This affirmation is the truly transformative moment in which the habitual is opened up to the yet unknown, the past is opened to the future.

    Written and performed by The Embassy
    Production and mix by Isak Eldh & The Embassy
    Artwork by Klas Barbrosson
    Released by Dream On 2023.
